Privacy Policy

How Megzier Group Ltd handles information collected through this website.

Overview

We collect only the information needed to respond to project inquiries, improve website performance, and maintain basic analytics. This typically includes the details you choose to share through the contact form or by email.

We do not sell personal data. Information is used for communication, service delivery, support, and simple site analytics.

Data Protection

Contact details sent through this website are stored only as long as needed to manage a project or answer a request. Access should remain limited to authorized staff working on your inquiry.

If you ask us to remove your contact details from our records, we will make a reasonable effort to do so unless we must keep them for legal, security, or accounting reasons.

Cookies

This website may use cookies or similar tools to measure traffic and improve performance. Those tools help us understand which pages get visited and how people move through the site.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings if you prefer. Some analytics or embedded features may work less well when cookies are disabled.
