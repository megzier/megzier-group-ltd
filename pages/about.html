Our Leadership

Meet the Founder & CEO

Eng. Robert Gitonga is the Chief Executive Officer and Managing Director of Megzier Group Ltd. A passionate technologist, engineer, and entrepreneur based in Nairobi, Kenya, Robert founded Megzier Group with one mission: make world-class technology accessible to every African business.

With deep hands-on expertise in website development, mobile application engineering, cybersecurity, enterprise software, network infrastructure, and digital transformation, Robert does not just manage — he builds. He is actively involved in every project, ensuring Megzier Group's technical and creative standards are upheld at all times.

Robert's work spans healthcare, travel and tourism, hospitality, international NGOs, and retail. Each project in his portfolio is not just a deliverable — it is a live, functioning digital product actively driving business growth for real clients right now.

Our Journey

How We Got Here

From a bold vision in Nairobi to serving clients across Kenya and internationally — the honest story of Megzier Group Ltd, milestone by milestone.

2024 — Before The Name
The Work Begins
Before Megzier Group was officially registered, Eng. Robert Gitonga was already building websites, configuring networks, and solving real technology problems for businesses across Nairobi. The demand grew faster than expected.
2025 — Taking Shape
First Major Client Projects Launched
The first major client websites were designed and launched — healthcare, hospitality, travel, and NGO platforms. Each project confirmed: African businesses deserve better technology partners.
March 2026 — Official Launch
Megzier Group Ltd is Registered
Megzier Group Ltd is officially incorporated as a Private Limited Company in Kenya. The company launches with 18 service categories, a registered office in Nairobi, and a clear mission to serve businesses at the highest level.
2026 — Version 1.0.0
Growing Portfolio & Expanding Services
The portfolio of live client websites grows across multiple industries. New services including AI integration, advanced cybersecurity, and enterprise ERP are added. International client inquiries begin arriving consistently.
2026 & Beyond
East Africa's Most Trusted IT Partner
The goal is clear: become the most reliable technology solutions company in East Africa — known not just for great code, but for the genuine business transformation we deliver for every client we work with.
