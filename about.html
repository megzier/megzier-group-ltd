Our Story

We Build Technology That Transforms African Business

Megzier Group Ltd is a registered Kenyan Private Limited Company delivering world-class IT solutions to businesses across Africa and internationally. We do not just build websites - we build digital engines that drive real business growth.

We exist to bridge the technology gap by delivering enterprise-grade digital solutions to Kenyan businesses, NGOs, healthcare providers, hospitality companies, and entrepreneurs at prices that make sense for our market.

Our Mission

Every business in Africa deserves access to the same quality of technology that powers Fortune 500 companies in New York and London. That is the belief that Megzier Group was built on.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become the most trusted IT solutions partner for businesses across East Africa and beyond - known for quality, reliability, speed, and genuine care for client success.

We are building a company that outlasts trends, adapts to every new technology, and always puts the success of our clients first. When you grow, we grow.

ENG. ROBERT GITONGA

CEO & Managing Director

Robert is the Chief Executive Officer and Managing Director of Megzier Group Ltd. A passionate technologist, engineer, and entrepreneur based in Nairobi, Kenya, Robert founded Megzier Group with one mission: make world-class technology accessible to every African business.

With hands-on expertise in website development, mobile application engineering, cybersecurity, enterprise software, network infrastructure, and digital transformation, Robert stays actively involved in every project from planning to launch.

Selected client work includes Mokawa Group, Zaya Travels and Tours, Eskin Medicare, Rachum International Foundation, FreePDFEdit, and MegzierAI Humanizer.
